Tips at the start

  • When Fighting monsters use your resources(their are explosives in some caves).
  • You can cook many types of food. Some can boost your speed, stealth, or health.
  • In places where it is colder you will need a torch.

Tips on weapons

  • Swords will BREAK if you use them a lot.
  • If you hold down the attack button you can do a powerful hit.
  • In the inventory you can check how strong your sword is.

Tips on hunting

  • Using arrows can be more effetive on birds.
  • Crouch down when you approch the animal to not be seen.
  • Stealth clothes are worth their cost when hunting.
